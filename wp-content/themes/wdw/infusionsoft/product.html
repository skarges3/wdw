Purpose Passion Plan Bracelet Collection

Wear these bracelets to remember who you are – and whose you are. This 3 bracelet set designed to wear stacked includes 1 link-style and 2 charm-style beaded bracelets in your choice of colors: Blush, Aquamarine and Bone.

Product Description

A woman who knows her purpose and passion, and has a plan for living and giving, finds outrageous joy and can change the world for God’s glory

The Women Doing Well Bracelet Collection is an artfully crafted reminder of a woman’s unique beauty and offering in this world on Christ’s behalf. The intertwined links represent the powerful relationships of women giving in community, the beauty of a woman who lives her purpose, passion and plan for God’s glory, and the biblical model of community found in the timeless truths of the trinity.

The Women Doing Well Bracelet Collection features pieces handcrafted by women artisans of Fashion & Compassion (link). Crocheted bead links from Eastern Europe, a hand-stamped charm from Mexico and crafted tagua beads from Ecuador are brought together by marginalized women in Charlotte, NC. Proceeds further the education of Fashion & Compassion artisans and their children around the world.

Link Style

Each bracelet is 7” long on an elastic cord of which there is 4” of 9-10 mm beads. The aquamarine and bone colored beads are hand-sanded tagua nut dyed with non-toxic vegetable dye from Ecuador. The blush color is sunstone from China. There are 3 - 1” links of crocheted seed beads in coordinating colors.

Charm Style

Each bracelet is 7” long of crocheted seed beads with a ¾” diameter WDW hand stamped logo charm. The charm for the blush and bone colored bracelet is bronze and the aquamarine beads have a sliver plated charm.
